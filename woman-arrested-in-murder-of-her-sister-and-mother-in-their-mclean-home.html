Woman arrested in murder of her sister and mother in their McLean home

Helen Hargan and her mother Pamela Hargan were shot to death in their McLean home in July of 2017.

MCLEAN, Va. -- A murder mystery in McLean may have been solved with an arrest. Police say the crime scene was staged to looked like a murder-suicide. Now, a woman is charged with killing both her sister and her mother.

Helen Hargan, 23, and her mother Pamela Hargan, 63, were shot to death in their McLean home on July 14, 2017.

Police first thought Helen shot her mother Pamela and then turned the gun on herself. Eventually, police say they realized it was really Helen’s sister Megan who killed them both.

And now, Megan Hargan, 35, has been charged with with two counts of first degree murder in the deaths of her own mother and sister. They were killed on July 14, 2017 in their McLean home on Dean Drive.

Carroll said detectives completed reconstruction of the crime scene and "interviewed Meghan Hargan twice."

On Thursday, Megan Hargan was indicted by a multi-jurisdictional grand jury, according to Carroll. She was arrested Friday morning in West Virginia after dropping off her daughter at school, according to sources.
