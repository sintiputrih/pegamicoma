Chelsea Clinton Goes Snorkeling With Diane von Furstenberg: Bikini Pic

Bon voyage! Chelsea Clinton and her husband, Marc Mezvinsky, spent Saturday, Aug. 15, boating with designer Diane von Furstenberg and her husband, Barry Diller, off the shore of Sardinia, Italy.

The new mom, 35, who welcomed daughter Charlotte in September 2014, looked fit in a mix-and-match bikini aboard the sleek vessel. 

Clinton’s outing without her mini-me comes as a rare moment for the busy first-time mom.

“Marc and I are like, ‘What did we do before we were parents?'” the former first daughter told Elle magazine in May. “My whole life is reoriented around my daughter in the most blessed sense. I now understand — this is something else that Marc and I talk about all the time — all of the enthusiastic, bombastically spectacular, wonderful things people say about their children, because we also feel and think all those things about Charlotte — that she is just the most remarkable little bubbly, perfect, chunky monkey creature ever.”
