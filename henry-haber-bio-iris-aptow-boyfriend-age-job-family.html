To mark the milestone, the couple also posted tributes on social media. “Happy anniversary my sweet,” Iris wrote on April 30 over a video of her and Ryder dancing, kissing, and snuggling. Likewise, Ryder posted a  snapshot of the pair sporting matching Tom and Jerry shirts, with a caption, “Happy anniversary my love.”

Unfortunately, we don’t know when the lovebirds call it quits.

Henry Haber Job

Henry Haber is a professional photographer and filmmaker. Admiring the beauties of creation from various walks of life, he’s inspired to cultivate his own style of expression through cinematography and media arts. He’s also influenced by his freelance photographer dad and designer mother to convey the arts as they serve as a necessity for understanding life.

But unlike most in his profession, Henry doesn’t like working alone. He always seeks to be a contributor to the goodness of the world and indeed feels that his purpose is to unify. In near future, Henry seeks to combine the meditative spirit required for surfing with the altruistic presence driven by his desire to connect to produce projects that are as inspirational as they are impactful.

So far, his works include Strangers, Imposter P., It All Starts with a Spark, Director’s Teaser Reel, Date Night, Dear Dad, The Final Chapter, Visit Santa Barbara Advertisement Spot, Love You More, JTV Advertisement Spot, KY Advertisement Spot, and X-Factor Basketball Program.

Besides working behind the camera, he also loves to pose in front of one. The last we checked, he was signed to the I am Models modeling agency.

As for his education, Henry is a Santa Barbara High School and Santa Barbara City College graduate.
