Professional Life & Career

She signed her first professional music contract, ‘Black River Entertainment,’ in 2013 when she was 19 years old. Her debut single, “Love Me Like You Mean It,” was released in October 2014. Kelsea has collaborated on the album with Josh Kerr and a few other writers. On the Billboard Country Airplay chart, this song reached number one. Chelsea became the first female country artist signed to an independent label to reach the top of the charts with this song.

Similarly, her album ‘The First Time,’ which was released in May 2015, featured the main track ‘Love Me As You Mean It.’ Kelsea released her debut self-titled EP in November 2014, which was well-received by critics. Kelsea’s popularity grew as a result of the album’s success.

Furthermore, Kelsea’s songs revealed a more mature side to her personality, and it seems that the majority of her songs were influenced by real-life events, such as her parent’s divorce and the heartbreaks she experienced. The first three singles from her debut studio album, “The First Time,” namely “CO,” “Peter Pan,” and “Love Me As You Mean It,” all charted at number one on the “Billboard Country Airplay” chart, making her the first new female artist to have her first three singles chart at number one. Furthermore, the album was well-received upon its initial release, but it was criticized for being too flirty. The single Peter Pan was a huge hit, and Kelsea even performed it at the Academy of Country Music Awards.

Kelsea became a sought-after artist following the success of her debut album.
