Will Devin Haney fight again in 2024 after losing to Ryan Garcia?

Devin Haney, the WBC super lightweight champion, suffered a significant setback in his career after his loss to Ryan Garcia in what many have termed as one of the best bouts of 2024.

The fight, which ended with a majority decision in Garcia's favor, has left the boxing world speculating on Haney's future in the sport.

The Impact of Haney's Loss

Haney's defeat at the hands of Garcia was a shocking turn of events, especially considering Haney's status as the favorite and an undefeated champion in multiple weight classes.

The bout highlighted significant vulnerabilities in Haney's performance, prompting questions about the legitimacy of his previously untarnished record and the fortitude of his chin.

Recovery Time and Potential Return

Jamaine Ortiz's Perspective

According to Jamaine Ortiz, a light welterweight contender, Haney may need an extended recovery period following the punishment he endured in the fight against Garcia.

Ortiz believes that Haney might not return to the ring until the first quarter of the following year due to the hard punches he absorbed during the match.

He expressed his concern for Haney's well-being and suggested that it could take six to nine months before Haney makes a comeback, citing the severity of the punishment he endured.

Trainer Robert Garcia's View

On the other hand, trainer Robert Garcia believes that Haney could potentially win a rematch with Garcia but should consider taking an easy tune-up fight before facing a high-caliber opponent or a rematch with Garcia.

He emphasizes the need for Haney to come back slowly from his loss to Garcia and suggests that a rematch between the two fighters should not happen until the summer of the following year.

Garcia also raises concerns about Haney's punch resistance, indicating that it may have been compromised, particularly since his fight against Jorge Linares in 2021.

Haney's Potential Path Forward

Possibility of a Rematch

Despite the loss, Haney has expressed his willingness to have a rematch with Garcia, indicating that he is open to the idea of redemption.

The potential for a rematch between Haney and Garcia has garnered significant attention from the boxing community, with many speculating on the outcome of a second encounter between the two fighters.

Mandatory Title Defense

Another factor that could influence Haney's decision to fight again in 2024 is his mandatory title defense against Sandor Martin, as stipulated by the World Boxing Council (WBC).

Haney's obligation to defend his title could impact the timing and nature of his next fight, potentially affecting his chances of engaging in a high-profile rematch with Garcia or pursuing other opportunities in the sport.

Considerations for Haney's Return

Physical and Mental Preparedness

Given the physical and mental toll of his loss to Garcia, Haney's return to the ring will likely hinge on his ability to recover from the punishment he endured and regain his confidence as a competitive boxer.

The extent to which Haney can address any vulnerabilities exposed in his previous fight will be crucial in shaping his readiness for future challenges in the ring.

Strategic Planning and Training

Haney's team and trainers will play a vital role in devising a strategic plan for his potential return to the ring.

This may involve careful consideration of his training regimen, sparring partners, and overall preparation to ensure that he is adequately equipped to compete at the highest level once again.

 

To conclude, the aftermath of Devin Haney's loss to Ryan Garcia has left the boxing world on edge, with questions surrounding Haney's future in the sport.

While opinions vary on whether Haney will fight again in 2024, factors such as recovery time, mandatory title defenses, and strategic considerations will undoubtedly influence his decision.

Devin Haney's journey back to the ring following his defeat at the hands of Ryan Garcia is poised to be a compelling storyline in the world of boxing, with fans and analysts alike eagerly anticipating his next steps in the sport.
