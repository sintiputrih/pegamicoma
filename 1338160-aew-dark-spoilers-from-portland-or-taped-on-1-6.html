AEW Dark Spoilers From Portland, OR (Taped On 1/6)

On January 6, All Elite Wrestling taped matches for before the live episode of Rampage and the Battle of the Belts V event at the Veterans Memorial Coliseum in Portland, Oregon.

The spoilers, courtesy of PWInsider, are as follows:

  • Kiera Hogan pinned Danika Della Rouge
  • Juice Robinson defeated Travis Williams
  • Ruby Soho & Willow Nightingale defeated Diamante & Emi Sakura
  • Lee Moriary & Big Bill defeated The Voros Twins (Chris & Patrick Voros)
  • Matt Hardy, Ethan Page & Isiah Kassidy defeated Judas Icarus, Sebastian Wolfe & Kaylen Tennedy
  • Brian Cage (with Prince Nana) defeated Carl Randers
  • Powerhouse Hobbs defeated Jayden.
  • ROH Champion Claudio Castagnoli & ROH Pure Champion Wheeler Yuta def. The Butcher & The Blade

After AEW Battle of the Belts V went off the air, AEW taped more matches for Dark. The spoilers, courtesy of PWInsider, are below.

  • Jake Hager def. Steve Miz
  • Tay Melo and Anna Jay A.S. def. Teal Piper (the daughter of Roddy Piper) & Kel
  • Sammy Guevara & Daniel Garcia def. Damos & Brian Cook
  • Angelo Parker & Matt Menard def. Eddie Curl & an unnamed partner; Parker and Menard attacked their opponents during their entrance.
  • ROH Women’s Champion Athena def. Marina Shafir (with Vickie Guerrero)
  • Action Andretti def. Ari Daivari

WrestleZone has coverage of AEW Dark as it airs every Tuesday.
