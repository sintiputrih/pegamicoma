Meet The Fiend On The Scene, WWEs Answer To Elf On The Shelf

As if Christmas wasn’t exciting enough, WWE has graced the WWE Universe with ‘The Fiend On The Scene’.

The Elf On The Shelf has been a popular item with kids the past few years, and WWEShop is now selling their own version of the doll modeled after “The Fiend” Bray Wyatt.

The listing, sounding more like an ominous warning than a warm getting, tells fans that “you never know when and where ‘The Fiend’ will pop up.”

Surprise your family and friends with this WWE Shop Exclusive plush doll. Get creative and any room into your very own Fun House!

The Fiend On The Scene Bray Wyatt Holiday Plush Toy stands 11″ Tall and it comes with Velcro hands, as well as a drawstring bag with “The Fiend” Bray Wyatt’s logo printed on it.

The plush normally retails for $19.99, but fans can pick it up now for 25% off through WWE’s Holiday Sale if they want to create holiday memories… or induce nightmares.
