Who is Dhanushs Spouse? Meet Aishwarya Rajinikanth

Dhanush is an Indian actor, producer, director, lyricist, and playback singer who predominantly works in Tamil cinema.

In 2011, Dhanush won the National Film Award for Best Actor for the Tamil film “Aadukalam” and in the same year, received international attention for his song “Why This Kolaveri Di”, which went on to become the most-viewed Indian song on YouTube. As of 2014, he has won five Filmfare Awards.

Over the next ten years, Dhanush featured in 25 movies and made his Hindi debut in “Raanjhanaa” in the year 2013. His first film was “Thulluvadho Ilamai”, a 2002 coming-of-age movie directed by his father, Kasthuri Raja.

Aishwarya Rajinikanth’s Biography

Aishwarya Rajinikanth born on 1 January 1982, is an Indian film director and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema. She is the eldest daughter of actor Rajinikanth and Lath a Rajinikanth and also the wife of actor Dhanush and made her feature film directorial debut with 3 (2012).

Aishwarya has a younger sister, Soundarya, who also works in the Tamil film industry. Aishwarya started her career in the Tamil film industry as a playback singer in the unreleased film Ramanaa, directed by Parthy Bhaskar. The song was composed by Deva in November 2000. Her first song released was “Natpe Natpe” from D. Imman’s soundtrack for Whistle (2003), which she sang with composer and actor Silambarasan.

In August 2011, she announced that she would be directing her husband, Dhanush and Shruti Haasan in his first feature film titled 3. Dhanush then explained that this would be his only film under the direction of his wife. The film was highly anticipated prior to its release, after the song “Why This Kolaveri Di” in the soundtrack went viral and became an internet phenomenon.

The song was composed by his cousin Anirudh Ravichander. She appeared alongside the main cast and composer during the making of the video. In July 2015, Aishwarya announced that she would soon launch a YouTube channel under the “Ten Entertainment” banner to exclusively promote original content and short films by aspiring filmmakers.

She served as a judge on STAR Vijay’s Jodi Number One dance competition in the third season along with members Sangeetha and Jeeva. She became a playback singer in the movie Whistle with Silambarasan, then the song “Un Mela Aasadhan” in the movie Aayirathil Oruvan, for which she also worked as an assistant director. In August 2016, Aishwarya was selected as India’s Goodwill Ambassador for UN Women.

In March 2017, Aishwarya performed Bharatanatyam at United Nations Headquarters on International Women’s Day but her performance was criticized on social media by professional Bharatanatyam dancers. Famous Indian dancer Anita Ratnam wrote on Aishwarya’s Facebook: “This is what Bharatanatyam becomes… A caricature and a farce!”

In December 2016, Aishwarya Rajinikanth released her own memoir Standing on An Apple Box: The Story of A Girl Among the Stars. With this book, she has revealed her own story of being a celebrity kid, making her career choices, her marriage, and her life as Rajinikanth’s daughter. Aishwarya is married to actor Dhanush and she has two sons, Yatra (born 2006) and Linga (born 2010). They announced their separation through their respective social media on January 17, 2022.
