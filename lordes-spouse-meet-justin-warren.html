Lorde’s Spouse: Meet Justin Warren. Lorde deliberately keeps her personal life hidden. Given this, it’s hardly surprising that she and her partner Justin Warren are among the quietest couples out there.

Who is Lorde’s Spouse?

The director of promotions for Universal Music in New Zealand is an American named Warren. Warren has collaborated with a number of well-known performers in addition to Lorde, such as Eminem, Justin Bieber, and Katy Perry.

Warren and Lorde originally met in 2009 when she signed with Universal Music. Yet, it took them almost six years before they started dating.

As of this writing, Warren is 42 years old, making him 17 years Lorde’s senior. James Lowe, a photographer, was Lorde’s previous lover and was seven years Lorde’s senior.

Sonja Yelich, Lorde’s mother, said at the beginning of 2014 that Lorde loved dating older men, whether or not her parents approved.

You won’t see any beautiful selfies with Warren since Lorde deleted all of her social media accounts in order to save her own mental health.

When Lorde and Warren were seen together in New Zealand in March 2019, the first engagement speculations were made. On her left hand, Lorde was sporting a silver ring at the moment.

The singer remained silent about the speculations, but in April 2021, when she was shown with a new magnificent gemstone on her ring finger, people started speculating about Lorde and Warren’s potential engagement once again.
