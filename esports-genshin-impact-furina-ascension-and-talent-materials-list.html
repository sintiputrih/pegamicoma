Genshin Impact: Furina Ascension and Talent Materials list

Furina, a five-star playable character, is supposedly coming to Genshin Impact in the 4.2 update. Hailing from Fontaine, she is a five-star hydro unit and wields a sword. The character has already made her debut in the game as an NPC. The community is looking forward to welcoming Furina to the game, as she is one of the seven archons and a great playable unit with a unique character mode. 

Genshin Impact’s latest drip marketing posts have all but confirmed the release of the Focalor in version 4.2 banners. While MiHoYo has not announced anything officially yet, we seem to have almost all the information needed, thanks to leaks by Honey Impact. To ensure that you can max out Furina as soon as she is released and experiment with her in different team compositions, this list contains her ascension and talent materials.

Materials List to Max Out Furina in Genshin Impact

Interested players can farm most resources available in the game’s current version based on the material leaks. Note that some resources, such as the boss ascension material, cannot be farmed until the 4.2 update is officially released.

Ascension List

Here is a list of all the materials you will need to farm out to max out Furina’s character levels in Genshin Impact.

  • Varunada Lazurite Silver – 1
  • Varunada Lazurite – 9
  • Varunada Lazurite Chunk – 9
  • Varunada Lazurite Gemstone – 6
  • Water that Failed to Transcend – 46
  • Lakelight Lily – 168
  • Whopperflower Nectar – 18
  • Shimmering Nectar – 30
  • Energy Nectar – 36

In addition to this, you will need about 7.1 million Mora and 419 Hero’s Wit for maximum ascension. Lakelight Lily and Water that Failed to Transcend will only be available after the 4.2 update drops.

Talent List

The following list contains the materials to max out Furina’s talent level –

  • Teachings of Justice – 9
  • Guide to Justice – 63
  • Philosophies of Justice – 114
  • Whopperflower Nectar – 18
  • Shimmering Nectar – 66
  • Energy Nectar – 93
  • 4.2 Weekly Boss Materials – 18
  • Crown of Insight – 3

The talent books can be farmed from Pale Forgotten Glory. The Pale Forgotten Glory is an underwater talent domain in the region of Fontaine, released in the 4.1 update. This domain only rewards Justice books on Tuesdays, Fridays, and Sundays. In the meantime, you can farm Whopperflowers, as their drops are essential for Furina’s ascension. The upcoming 4.2 weekly boss will also drop materials that will be needed to max out Furina’s abilities.
