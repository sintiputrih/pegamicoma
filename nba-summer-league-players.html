Who Plays In The NBA Summer League? Schedule 2023

Detroit Pistons Summer League Roster 2023 includes Ausar Thompson, Tosan Evbuomwan, Amar Sylla and Marcus Sasser.

Rookies, sophomores, undrafted prospects and G League affiliate plays in the NBA Summer League. The 2023 tournament will run from July 7-17.

The Summer League is a unique off-season competition highlighting the potential of basketball's up-and-coming players, including those recently drafted into the NBA.

Wonder kids such as Victor Wembanyama, Jaden Hardy, and Dyson Daniels will be in action. It is an excellent opportunity to exhibit their skills and secure a spot on their team's regular season lineup.

You can catch the 2023 NBA Summer League on TV channels like ESPN, ESPN2, and ESPNU. If you own NBA League Pass, you can stream all the Summer League games shown on NBA TV.

NBA Summer League Players To Watch

Players worth keeping an eye on during the 2023 NBA Summer League include:

  • Victor Wembanyama
  • MarJon Beauchamp
  • Dyson Daniels
  • Jaden Hardy
  • Shaedon Sharpe
  • Paolo Banchero
  • Patrick Baldwin Jr.
  • James Wiseman
  • Bennedict Mathurin
  • Keegan Mitchell

Watching Summer League games is always a delight, mainly because teams typically feature a roster of new draft picks and youthful players. Observing how these young athletes will perform and contribute to their respective teams is intriguing.

Victor Wembanyama summer league 2023 participation is expected to be a major attraction. Wembanyama will be carefully watched by many since he is the leading prospect in the upcoming 2023 NBA Draft.

The highly-rated French center standing at 7'4", has the ability to become a game-changing player for any team lucky enough to acquire him. Many experts predict he will be the top pick for the San Antonio Spurs in the 2023 NBA draft.

Besides, promising 2022 picks MarJon Beauchamp, Shaedon Sharpe, and Jaden Hardy are players to watch in the upcoming summer league. Beauchamp, the 6'7" Milwaukee Bucks small forward, is a high-energy player who can defend multiple positions.

Similarly, in the 2022 NBA draft, Hardy was selected by the Sacramento Kings at the 37th position and later traded to the Dallas Mavericks on the same night. The 20-year-old played 48 games for Dallas in the 2022-23 season averaging 8.8 points per game.

Another exciting name, Shaedon Sharpe, is a Canadian native who plays for the Portland Trail Blazers. Sharpe scored 30 points, grabbed 7 rebounds, and made 7 assists against the Kings in March 2023.

Those were impressive numbers in a game to be accomplished by a teenager, and only LeBron James and Kevin Durant have achieved such a remarkable feat.
