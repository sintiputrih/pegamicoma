Jadagrace is an American actress, dancer, and singer. She appeared in the 2009 film, Terminator Salvation, and also has her own television series, The Jadagrace Show. Her song, "Run Dat Back", was included as a "new artist" track on volume 42 of Now That's What I Call Music in 2012 and released her first single entitled "Express Yourself" when she was nine years old. She was signed to a record deal in 2012 after being discovered by Redfoo of LMFAO, and released her first single Run Dat Back under the Epic Records music label. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
