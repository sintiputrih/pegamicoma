Freya Allan The Witcher

Netflix has cast several new actors including the two lead female roles in their upcoming fantasy series “The Witcher”. Freya Allan is set to take on the role of Ciri and Anya Chalotra will play the character Yennefer. They both join the previously cast Henry Cavill, who is playing Geralt. Yennefer is a powerful sorceress and the primary love interest of Geralt while Ciri is the princess of Cintra, whose role is central to the series’ plot. Both Geralt and Yennefer serve as parental figureheads to the young Ciri.

The other cast of the Netflix series include Jodhi May (Game of Thrones) as Queen Calanthe; Bjorn Hlynur Haraldsson (Fortitude) as the knight Eist; Adam Levy (Knightfall) as the druid Mousesack; MyAnna Buring (Downton Abbey) as Tissaia, the head of the magical academy at Aretuza; Mimi Ndiweni (The Legend of Tarzan) and Therica Wilson-Read (Suicide Club) as novice sorcerers; and Millie Brady (King Arthur: Legend of the Sword) as the outcast Princess Renfri.

The NetFlix series is based on the fantasy novel series by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski. The books also inspired the incredible video game franchise by Warsaw-based game studio CD Projekt Red.
