Kevin Hart's Pregnant Wife Eniko Shares a Gorgeous Selfie Flaunting Her Natural Curly Hair

Eniko Hart is glowing in recent pictures shared to her Instagram account, as she and husband Kevin Hart prepare to welcome the new addition to their family.

Kevin Hart’s wife of three years is expecting their second child and Kevin’s fourth, and she’s sporting a beautiful pregnancy glow in every picture shared to her Instagram account.

Kevin Hart and Eniko Parrish attend 2019 Roc Nation THE BRUNCH on February 9, 2019 in Los Angeles, California. | Photo by Vivien Killilea/Getty Images for Roc Nation

On July 30, Eniko left fans in awe as she posted a selfie showing off her luscious curly hair and her natural-looking makeup. The mother of one seemed to be inside a car, sporting a black top, a pair of necklaces, and her seatbelt in place. “I come alive in the summer time,” she captioned the pic.

The 35-year-old has been sharing her workout routines with fans to show them not only how she stays active in her third trimester, but also how she spends her time amid the quarantine that has obligated families around the world to isolate in their homes.

At the start of July, Eniko admitted in an Instagram post that the third trimester was starting to take a toll on her, and she was visibly slowing down while working out. However, she mentioned that the bright side of the situation is she has become a “fan of all shades of pink.”

Although she’s the mother of his kids, Eniko can’t escape the comic’s shenanigans.

Eniko’s mention of the color pink was a reference to the sex of her baby, which she and Kevin revealed alongside his older kids, Heaven and Hendrix, from his marriage to ex-wife Torrei Hart.

“I literally screamed, laughed, and cried when we found out this time around because she & Kenzo are what I’ve always prayed for,” Eniko said about finding out she was having a baby girl.

The expecting mother added that she’s anxious to meet her daughter, whom she claims will boss everyone around alongside her brother Kenzo—Eniko and Kevin’s 2-year-old son.

Kevin Hart, on the other hand, has been enjoying witnessing his wife’s pregnancy up-close thanks to the lockdown, and although she’s the mother of his kids, Eniko can’t escape the comic’s shenanigans.

A few weeks ago, Kevin caught a disheveled Eniko lying on the couch with one leg propped up and the other down as she watched TV. Eniko was wearing blue shorts, a grey top that put her baby bump on display, and a pair of Nike flip flops.

Kevin joked saying his wife would “kill him” for posting a picture of her in that state, but mentioned the view was too funny not to share, Luckily for him, Eniko took the pic with humor, stating that she has “no shame in my game.”
