Who Is C.J Beathard?

Casey Jarrett, also known as “C. J.” Beathard is a football quarterback for the Jacksonville Jaguars.

Born on November 16, 1993, Beathard played football as a college student at the University of Iowa and redshirted during his freshman year.

Before then, he played football in high school for the Wildcats, at Battle Ground Academy in Franklin, Tennessee.

In 2013, Beathard appeared in five games for the Hawkeyes.

His collegiate debut was against Western Michigan and when he played against the Broncos, he finished with a 54-yard and 4 rushes for 30 yards.

His first career touchdown came on a five-yard rush when he was matched against Purdue.

In 2017, Beathard was drafted by the San Francisco 49ers, in the third round of the NFL Draft.

During a game with the 49ers in 2020, Beathard threw for 182 yards and three touchdowns.It became his second winning game in the NFL.

The Jacksonville Jaguars had signed in Beathard in 2021, on a two-year contract worth $5 million.
