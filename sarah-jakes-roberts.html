Sarah Jakes is a motivational speaker and self-help author known for her memoirs Lost & Found, Dear Mary, and Colliding With Destiny. Following the success of these works, she appeared as a guest on shows including Dr. Phil (2002), Exhale (2013), and Today (2014).
