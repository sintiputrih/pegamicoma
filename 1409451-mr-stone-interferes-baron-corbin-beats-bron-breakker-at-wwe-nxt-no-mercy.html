Mr. Stone Interferes, Baron Corbin Beats Bron Breakker at WWE NXT No Mercy

Baron Corbin scored the victory at WWE NXT No Mercy.

Bron Breakker and Baron Corbin waged war at WWE NXT No Mercy. The two powerhouses have been engaged in a bitter rivalry in recent weeks, and the bad blood boiled over on the September 26 episode of NXT. There, Corbin and Breakker brawled throughout the show, and they even crashed through a wall in Shawn Michaels’ office.

Breakker and Corbin kicked off NXT No Mercy, and Corbin had a special entrance, as he rode a motorcycle to the ring.

Breakker dove onto Corbin to jump-start the action. The match was a hard-hitting brawl from start to finish, as the two foes duked it out. Breakker took everything Corbin had at him; he even sat up after Corbin slammed him through the broadcast table.

In the end, Mr. Stone came out and tried to get involved. Breakker tossed him out of the ring onto some security guards, but Corbin capitalized by hitting Breakker with the End of Days for the win.

Breakker previously took Von Wagner, Mr. Stone’s client, out with a vicious attack.
