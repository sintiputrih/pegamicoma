Their Beginning

Kyrie’s parents met each other back in the summer of 2012. At the time, Damien and Biannca both worked at a Little Caesars Pizza restaurant in their hometown, Fort Wayne, Indiana. They were just co-workers for a long time before they began dating.

The lovers then started to encounter various issues and some were related to their financial situation. At one point, the couple was completely broke and had to leave their apartment and move into a motel.

After their first son, DJ was born, Kyrie’s mother once had to ask her mother for money so she could purchase food.

It was Kyrie’s father, Damien’s decision to start a YouTube channel. He hoped that it would garner people’s attention and relieve at least some of their financial woes.
