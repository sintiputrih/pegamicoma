North West and Penelope Disick 'Moana' Birthday Party

On Saturday, North West and Penelope Disick celebrated their birthdays luau style—complete with leis and colorful skirts—with a Moana-themed party. The four-year-old cousins were only born three weeks apart, so it was a must for the two to have a joint summer celebration.

The tots went all out in costume. North wore a tropical printed two piece, flower crown and crochet belt; while Penelope channeled Moana's outfit as she posed for a picture with Sophia, Larsa Pippen's daughter.

And since the Kardashians are not ones to party subtly, the birthday bash also included a pig petting zoo, shaved ice, a bounce house and more. The tots not only got matching Pomeranian puppies, but also matching three-tier Disney-themed cakes.

It seemed everyone had a blast, parents included. Kourtney Kardashian brought out her inner child when she rode a mechanical surfboard in a bounce house with two of her friends. "The only three grown-ups (sort of) to go on," she captioned the photo.

In another snap, Kourtney posed with friend Stephanie Shepherd and some shaved ice.

Meanwhile, North showed off her dance moves in front of her family and friends.

And Penelope was all smiles as she hung out with a Moana look-alike in her cheetah print bathing suit. She toted her new puppy and wore long brown clip-ins for the event.

According to her aunt Khloé's Instagram, North is also apparently enjoying her new dog too.
