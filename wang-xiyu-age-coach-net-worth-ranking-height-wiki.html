Wang Xiyu Age, Coach, Net worth, Ranking, Height, Wiki

Wang Xiyu is a Chinese tennis player. On August 15, 2022, she reached her career-high singles ranking of world No.75. On May 27, 2019, she peaked at No.240 in the WTA doubles rankings.

Wang made her ITF Women’s Circuit debut in 2016 at the $10K event in Anning as a wildcard player. Despite the loss in the first match, the following week, she reached the semifinal at the $10K event in the same city.

At the 2022 Budapest Grand Prix, she defeated top seed Barbora Krejcikova in the first round and overcame Ana Bogdan in the longest straight sets match of the year in 2 hours and 45 minutes. On August 31, 2022, Wang defeated 3rd-seeded M Sakkari to enter the 3rd round of the US Open tennis tournament in NY.
