Heidi Klum Seen With Ex-Boyfriend Martin Kirsten After Split: Picture

Looks like Heidi Klum and her ex-boyfriend Martin Kirsten still have some unfinished business between them. Just eight days after a source confirmed to Us Weekly that the couple had gone their separate ways, the supermodel and her former bodyguard beau were spotted leaving a building together in L.A.'s Brentwood neighborhood.

The 40-year-old Project Runway host dressed casually for the outing in black pants, black Ugg boots, and a loose, graphic-print T-shirt. Kirsten, meanwhile, wore jeans, a black shirt, and a New York Yankees hoodie. Per X17, neither spoke as they walked to their respective cars. (See more photos of the pair's reunion.)

As previously reported, the couple called it quits recently after more than a year of dating. "He is no longer working with her or her family," an insider told Us.
