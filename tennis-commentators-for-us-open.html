Who are the commentators for the US Open 2023?

From left: Pam Shriver, Patrick McEnroe, Cliff Drysdale and Chris Fowler(Image via Sports Business Journal/NDTV Sports/Internations tennis Hall of Fame/The Ringer)

The 143rd edition of the US Open Championship, which will commence on the 28th of August 2023, has announced its list of commentators. The qualifiers for the event are already in play as players make their way into the opening round. The final matches are scheduled for 10 September. The event will be held at the USTA Billie Jean King National Tennis Center, New York, USA as per norms.

This Grand Slam will be one for the books. With Novak Djokovic fighting for his 24th Major title and Carlos Alcaraz set out on his campaign to defend his crown, it will be a sight to behold. Flushing Meadows will serve as the big closer for all Grand Slam events this season.

The tournament will be broadcasted on different platforms in different regions.

Commentating in today’s fast-paced game comprises a lot of factors. While the live commentary itself proves a vital part, it also includes a plethora of other elements. This also involves post-match analysis and previews for a match. The following list comprises the elite team of broadcasters and analysts for the US Open.

Commentators:

  • Cliff Drsydale
  • Chris Fowler
  • Patrick McEnroe
  • Pam Shriver

Analysts:

  • Darren Cahill
  • Chris Evert
  • Mary Joe Fernandez
  • Brad Gilbert
  • Jason Goodall
  • Alexandra Stevenson
  • Caroline Wozniacki
  • James Blake
  • Rennae Stubbs

The team will also contain Chris McKendry as the host and Jeremy Schaap as the reporter.
