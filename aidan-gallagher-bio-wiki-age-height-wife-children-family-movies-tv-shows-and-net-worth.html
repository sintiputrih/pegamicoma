Aidan Gallagher Actor

Gallagher rose to fame in 2013 with his role as Alec in the TV series ‘Modern Family. Gallagher also appeared in the film ‘Jacked Up’ in 2013, where he played the role of Evan and portrayed the lead character Nicky Harper in the TV series ‘Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn’ in 2014.

The comedy serial, which began off with a 20-episode first season, was followed by a 25-episode second season and a 24-episode third season. The serial was renewed for season 4 in 2017 by Nickelodeon.

The series follows the adventures of the Harper quadruplets played by Aidan, Mace Coronel, Casey Simpson, and Lizzy Greene who don’t have much in common but know they can do anything when they are together. Michael and Matt Fleckenstein had developed the comedy.
