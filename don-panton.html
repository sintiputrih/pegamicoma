Don Panton is a Bahamian social media star who got massive popularity from the YouTube channel, Panton’s Squad. Don Panton is best known for comprising one-half of the Panton’s Squad YouTube channel.

Early Life and Childhood

Don Panton first opened his eyes on December 13, 1984. Presently, his age is 38 years old and his birth sign is Sagittarius.

He was born in the Commonwealth of The Bahamas, a coral-based archipelago in the Atlantic Ocean. He was only two years old when he moved to the United States with his family. His father’s name is Donald Panton.

Moreover, he has relatives in Jamaica. He is a huge sports fan and watches ESPN most of the time. His favorite rapper is Lil Wayne. He is scared of heights and has trouble traveling by plane.

Don Panton’s wife

Don and his wife, Malinda first met in 2006. They dated for a year before marrying. The two began talking online before finally meeting in person and first went on a double date at the PGA Movie Theatre in Palm Beach, Florida.

Professional Life

Don is best known for uploading pranks, challenges, reactions, comedy videos, and vlogs on his channel, Pantons Squad. Moreover, he gained widespread popularity there for his couples’ vlogging, including pranks and challenges, alongside his wife Malinda Panton.

Therefore, it was Malinda who first introduced him to YouTube in early 2017 and convinced him to create a channel with her.

After getting a camera as a gift, they jointly created the channel ‘ Panton’s Squad’ on January 5, 2017, to post vlogs, prank, and challenge videos featuring the whole family including their two kids.

Moreover, their first video, ‘Season 1 Ep.1- Meet The Pantons Squad- family vlog’, in which the couple introduced themselves, was posted two days later on January 7, 2017.

Furthermore, the video, ‘Daughter Cuts Off Her Natural Hair Prank!’, posted on June 29, 2017, became viral and earned over 1 million views within a couple of weeks.

He uploaded a video on August 1st celebrating the feat, in which he asked his two kids questions about himself to see who knew him better.

Moreover, by this time, he had also created three more YouTube channels for their kids. Moreover, ‘Sister Brother Fun Toys’ was created in February 2017, followed by ‘Yaya Panton’ in May and ‘Yaya and Dj Fun Kids’ in July 2017.

Further, his main channel took about a year to cross the one million subscribers mark.
