Pastor Elisha Wanted Me To Pretend That I Am Under The Influence Of Marine Spirits To Lure More Memb

Few days ago, Ghanaian “celebrity” Pastor known as Pastor Prince Elisha Osei Kofi hit the airwaves after giving death related prophecies to some celebrities who visited his church.

Pastor Elisha Prophesied to TV personality Afia Schwarzenegger and comic actor Oteele. Lots of controversies has sprung up ever since his video went viral.

One of such controversy is the recent revelation Ghanaian songstress cum entrepreneur Mzbel disclosed about Prophet Elisha.

According to Mzbel in an interview with Zionfelix on the uncut show, Pastor Elisha offered her 10,000 cedis to agree to a fake prophecy that Marine Spirits are the responsible for her inability to get married because she was wedded to the marine spirits in the spiritual realm.

It would be recalled that when Mzbel first made these accusations, she didn’t mention the pastor’s name until her interview with Zionfelix. Afia Schwarzenegger even took a swipe at her and called her all sort of names for spewing lies about the man of God.

But to make her accusations clear Mzbel stated emphatically that when Pastor Elisha made the proposal, she suggested that they do something believable and not a lie but the man of God rejected that request, which was why she failed to attend his church.

Mzbel  further explained that Pastor Elisha sort her help in expanding his church and as kind hearted as she was she was willing to help him out until  he made the proposal.

Asked why she has decided to speak out after staying quiet all this while,  Mzbel told Zionfelix that she decided to make these revelations known because she wants people to see the sort of person Prophet Elisha was, especially, after his latest prophesy to Afia Schwarzenegger in his church which suggested that she (Mzbel) and Nana Tonardo are planning to poison her.
