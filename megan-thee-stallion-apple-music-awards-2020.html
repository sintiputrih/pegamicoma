See Megan Thee Stallion Light Up a Rodeo for 2020 Apple Music Awards

During last night's 2020 Apple Music Awards, Megan Thee Stallion put on an explosive, showstopping performance in an empty Houston rodeo, performing fan favorites like "Realer," "Girls in the Hood," "Cash Sh*t," "Don't Stop," "Savage," and "Body," according to Entertainment Tonight Canada.

The rapper received the award for Breakthrough Artist of the Year at the ceremony. "I am so excited to be the Breakthrough Artist of the Year. This is a really big accomplishment for me," she said, per ET Canada. "Apple Music has been rocking with me for so long, even before making me an Up Next artist, and I am just very appreciative and very grateful. I will forever love ya'll as much as ya'll love me."

In one particularly sizzling clip, Megan wears a golden ensemble composed of a matching cowboy hat, a bodysuit, gloves, knee-high boots, and a sheer cape. Behind her, a giant star is lit on fire, while cowboys circle her on horseback.

In another clip, Megan performs on the empty bleachers as an entourage of dancers surround her.

Last month, Megan dropped her latest album, Good News. "Hotties , I first want to say thank you for riding with me, growing with me, and staying down with me since my first mixtape Rich Ratchet !" she wrote, referring to her 2016 mixtape, to fans on Instagram at the time. "Through this rough ass year we’ve all been having I felt like we could all use a lil bit of good news."
