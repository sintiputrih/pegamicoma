How old is Kyle Shanahan?

Kyle Shanahan, the renowned American football coach, was born on December 14, 1979, in Minneapolis, Minnesota, making him 44 years old as of February 2024.

Early Life and Education

Kyle Shanahan attended Saratoga High School in 1994 while his father worked as the offensive coordinator for the San Francisco 49ers. Later, he transferred to Cherry Creek High School in Colorado, where his father served as the head coach of the Denver Broncos. After high school, he chose to join the University of Texas at Austin as a redshirt freshman, where he played as a wide receiver.

Career in Football

After graduating from college in 2003, Shanahan became a graduate assistant to Karl Dorrell at UCLA. He then began his coaching career with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers as an assistant coach for offensive quality control under head coach Jon Gruden. Subsequently, he joined the Houston Texans in 2006, working with former football coach Gary Kubiak and serving as their wide receivers coach. In January 2008, he was officially promoted as the youngest coordinator in the NFL.

In 2010, Shanahan joined his father, who was working with the Washington Redskins at the time. From 2014 to January 2015, he served as the offensive coordinator for the Cleveland Browns. In the same month, he became the new offensive coordinator for the Atlanta Falcons. In the 2016 season, he was named the NFL Assistant Coach of the Year after the Falcons' offense achieved notable success.

Since 2017, Shanahan has been serving as the head coach for the San Francisco 49ers, leading the team to several victories.

Personal Life

Kyle Shanahan is happily married to his wife Mandy, whom he met at Cherry Creek High School in Colorado. They tied the knot on July 5, 2005, and are the proud parents of three children: Stella, Carter, and Lexi Louise.

Physical Attributes

Shanahan possesses an athletic body type, standing at 6 feet 2 inches (1.88 meters) tall and weighing approximately 81 kg. He has dark brown hair and eyes of the same color.

As of February 2024, Kyle Shanahan is 44 years old and continues to make significant contributions to the world of American football as the head coach for the San Francisco 49ers.
