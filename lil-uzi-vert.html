    Lil Uzi Vert’s Facts

  • Lil Uzi Vert uses “they/them” pronouns.
  • In their interviews, they have credited their school friend William Aston for introducing them to rapping. They had heard Aston freestyling and had approached him. Aston encouraged them to give it a try.
  • They were discovered by producer and DJ Don Cannon after he heard their track on the radio played by DJ Diamond Kuts. Cannon passed a word about their talent to DJ Drama and they signed up Lil Uzi for their record label, Generation Now.
  • They decided to adopt the Lil Uzi moniker because of their rapping style. One of their friends had told them that they rapped like a machine gun and they decided to adapt Lil Uzi as their stage name.
  • They have often stated in their interview that they started rapping just for money. They had no particular interest in it. In fact, they have claimed in some interviews that they hate rapping.
  • In May 2017, the police authorities arrested a teenager in California after he threatened to shoot up his high school if Lil Uzi didn’t release Luv is Rage 2 album.
  • In December 2016, they were arrested by the authorities in Atlanta for several traffic violations while riding a dirt bike. They were also accused of speeding away from police and trying to flee on foot after they had lost balance on the bike.
  • During their early struggling days, they used to work as a stock person at the Bottom Dollar store. After they quit their job, their mother kicked them out of the house.
  • They got the word “Faith” tattooed on their forehead so that they would focus only on their rap career. Their logic was that they can’t go for a job interview with that face tattoo.
  • They are particularly fond of face tattoos. They have got ‘God is king’ tattooed in a circle below their left eye.
  • On March 6, 2020, they released their 2nd studio album, Eternal Atake, which included songs like Baby Pluto, You Better Move, Homecoming, Chrome Heart Tags, I’m Sorry, and Secure the Bag. The album was released via Generation Now and Atlantic Records as a surprise release and debuted atop the US Billboard 200, becoming Lil Uzi Vert’s second US number-one album.
  • They have also got a number of Adinkra symbols from the Asante tribe of West Africa tattooed on their face. They have no ancestral connection with the tribe.
  • In February 2021, Lil Uzi showcased a $24 million pink diamond of 10 and 11 carats attached to their forehead. They even revealed that they had spent almost 4 years to pay for the $24 million diamond and it was by their favorite jewelry designer, Elliot Eliantte.
  • In October 2021, they released a new single titled Demon High.
  • In July 2022, Lil Uzi released a new EP titled Red & White.
  • In June 2023, they released a new studio album titled Pink Tape.
