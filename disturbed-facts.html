David Draiman’s Early Life and Career

David Draiman was born on March 13, 1973, in Brooklyn, New York. His family moved to Chicago when he was young, and it was there that he began his music career. Draiman graduated from Loyola University Chicago and began working in the healthcare industry. However, music was always his passion, and he soon left his day job to pursue his dreams.

David’s Height and Relationships

Standing at an impressive 6’6″, Draiman is a towering presence both on and off stage. He has been married twice, first to a woman named Lena Yada, and later to model and actress, Lena Katina. Draiman has one son named Samuel with his current wife, Lena Katina.

Disturbed’s Career

Disturbed was formed in 1994, and their debut album, “The Sickness,” was released in 2000. It was an immediate success, reaching #20 on the Billboard 200 chart and going on to sell over 4 million copies in the United States alone. The album featured hit songs like “Stupify” and “Down with the Sickness,” which remain fan favorites to this day.

Since then, Disturbed has released seven more studio albums, each one more successful than the last. They have sold over 17 million records worldwide and have been nominated for multiple Grammy awards.
