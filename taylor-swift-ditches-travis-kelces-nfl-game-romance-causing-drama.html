Taylor Swift Ditches Travis Kelces NFL Game As Their Romance Stirs Drama With His Loved One

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift's absence from Travis Kelce's recent game has sparked speculation about drama in their relationship and within Travis's inner circle.
  • NFL commentators confirmed that Taylor Swift was not at the game, leaving some brokenhearted fans in the stands.
  • Two of Travis Kelce's ex-girlfriends, Kayla Nicole and Maya Benberry, seem to have a problem with his romance with Taylor, causing drama in his inner circle.

Taylor Swift was previously pictured getting close to Travis Kelce’s mom at one of his Chiefs game and has even brought her own posse of famous pals to attend as well. However, even though things have appeared to be heating up between the pair until now, a new report says that their relationship may be causing drama in Travis’ inner circle.

Moreover, this theory seems to be supported by Taylor’s recent absence from Travis’ game, which NFL commentators went on to confirm for curious fans.

It’s Unclear Why Taylor Avoided Travis’ Sunday Game

Despite many viewers hoping to see the 33-year-old singer cheering for Travis and his team during their game against the Vikings at U.S. Bank Stadium in Minneapolis on Sunday, October 8, NFL announcers confirmed Taylor’s absence shortly after the game started.

“In case you’re wondering, seven words I thought would never leave my lips in my career, what are they? Taylor Swift is not at the game,” commentator Jim Nantz revealed to viewers.

“There are some brokenhearted people I just saw in the stands,” he continued. “She was questionable all week, but it looks like it’s officially inactive for today.”

In the two Sundays before, Swift was spotted in the stadium stands, making the trip to Minnesota specifically to see her rumored beau play. This included Travis’ game at Arrowhead Stadium during the Chiefs' victory over the Chicago Bears on September 24.

During the appearance, Taylor notably engaged in conversation with the athlete’s mother, Donna Kelce, within the tight end's exclusive suite and exhibited excitement, leaping up and down when he scored a touchdown.

A mere week later, Taylor gathered a star-studded group of her celebrity pals (including Blake Lively, Ryan Reynolds, Sophie Turner, Hugh Jackman, and Sabrina Carpenter). Together, they showed their support for Travis as the Chiefs secured a close victory against the New York Jets at MetLife Stadium in New Jersey.

Travis And Taylor’s Romance Is Causing Drama In His Inner Circle

While things appeared to be going well between Travis and Taylor until now, one report says that their romance has caused drama within Travis’ inner circle.

As Fox News points out, two of Travis’ ex-girlfriends seemingly have a problem with his blossoming romance with Taylor. Until last year, Travis was dating sports commentator Kayla Nicole, who he’s faced rumors of cheating on in the past. The pair dated on and off from 2017 to 2022, and during that time, Kayla got close with some of the other wives and girlfriends of NFL players, including Brittany Mahomes, who’s married to Patrick Mahomes.

However, it was recently spotted by eagled-eyed fans that Kayla had unfollowed Brittany, after she was seen going out to dinner with Taylor as well as hanging with the pop star during one of Travis’ games, seeming to prove there’s bad blood between the women.

Prior to that, Travis’ ex-girlfriend Maya Benberry issued a public warning to Taylor, claiming he had cheated during their brief relationship in 2016. For now, Travis hasn’t responded to the drama involving his ex-girlfriends.
