Football career and injury of Cooper Manning

Isidore Newman School located in New Orleans, was where Cooper Manning played and he excelled as a wide receiver for their football team.

Although on the verge of a very promising college career, his football career would hit a very large snag during his junior season when he was diagnosed with spinal stenosis, a condition where the spinal canal narrows, putting pressure on the spinal cord and nerve roots.
