LeToya Luckett's Husband Tommicus Shows off His Two Daughters Chilling Outdoors in Sunglasses

LeToya Luckett's husband Tommicus Walker recently shared a snap of his girls Gianna and Madison seated on big stones: "Daddy's little girls."

In 2019, LeToya Luckett became a first-time mom to a daughter, Gianna, while her husband Tommicus Walker became a second-time girl dad. The singer's spouse already had a child, Madison, from a previous relationship.

The couple has since become proud parents, often sharing snaps of their children on social media. Recently, Walker shared a sweet post of his two girls bonding outdoors.

In the photo, both Gianna and Madison rocked a pair of reflective sunglasses while sitting on the edge of a low stone wall and sharing a bag of chips. "Daddy's little girls," he captioned the post.

Madison looked laid back in a black Versace top, black leggings, and chunky white sneakers that matched her little sister's. Meanwhile, the one-year-old looked adorable in a beige jumper and a white long-sleeved shirt underneath.

The former Destiny's Child member announced her pregnancy with Gianna in 2018 and kept her fans updated through social media. Madison also played a big part in the revelation after giving clues in an Instagram video.

Ludacris' wife and Luckett's best friend, Eudoxie Bridges, was one of the two matrons of honor in the wedding.

Shortly after giving birth, Luckett shared a black and white snap of her newborn's hand clinging to her finger. "Our lives are forever changed," she captioned the post.

Little Gianna came two years after Luckett and Walker tied the knot in a lavish ceremony at Villa Antonia, Texas. The bride then donned a custom-made Vera Wang gown with Christian Louboutin shoes while the groom rocked a Tom Ford jacket.

Ludacris' wife and Luckett's best friend, Eudoxie Bridges, was one of the two matrons of honor in the wedding, while Walker's brothers, Tamarcus Joe and Treoun, were the best men.

The formal event had a black, white, and deep red color scheme, with the bridesmaids donning differently styled black gowns. Guests included singers Michelle Williams, Tank, and Ludacris.

On September 14, 2020, Luckett and Walker welcomed another addition to their growing family, a baby boy named Tysun Wolf Walker. The singer immediately shared an adorable photo of her newborn resting on her lap in an Instagram photo.

Similarly, her husband took to Instagram snaps of their little boy looking comfortable on a furry blanket and another featuring Tysun's curly hair in a close-up angle.
