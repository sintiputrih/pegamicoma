Nikki Glaser Was Heartbroken After She Was Featured In Taylor Swift: Miss Americana

Nikki Glaser was a perfect guest for The Howard Stern Show most recently. She had a lot to discuss, which included what went on behind the scenes during the Tom Brady roast, and the unaired moments. In addition, she discussed how stand-up comedy saved her life, and her relationship with Chris Convy that has been on and off for a decade.

What tends to be a frequent topic of conversation on The Howard Stern Show is Taylor Swift. She was once again brought up with Nikki Glaser, but the topic had lots of meaning for the comedian. She's a huge Swiftie, but was featured in Taylor Swift's life for all the wrong reasons. Glaser's voice was in a compilation during Miss Americana, basically shading Taylor Swift and her friends. Glaser was heartbroken at the moment.

She tells Howard Stern, “I was mouthing off on some interview about her in a way that was not kind, but it was me being jealous and feeling like I couldn’t be friends with her,” Nikki told Howard. “Then it comes out in the documentary that she was anorexic. I was like, ‘What the f***?’”

Glaser concludes, “I was commenting on somebody’s body who has the same f****** thing I did. It was so grotesque, so I issued an apology on Instagram."

Nikki Glaser Spent Thousands On Taylor Swift's Eras Tour, Attending 12 Concerts

12 different cities, and $25,000 was spent by Nikki Glaser during Taylor Swift's Eras tour. This all took place after Nikki decided against freezing her eggs at the last minute. Given all the costs she saved, Glaser decided to spoil herself with the added funds.

“I was [considering] freezing my eggs in case I break up with my boyfriend — who I don’t plan on breaking up with — and I meet a guy who refuses to adopt … and then I felt sad about it, so I said, ‘I’m not doing this’ … and that’s when the tour was announced,” Nikki told Howard.

Nikki Glaser Posted A Lengthy Apology On Instagram After Bashing Taylor Swift

Not only did Glaser feel awful about being included in the compilation, but in 2020, the comedian took to Instagram, posting an apology about being included in the Miss Americana documentary.

She wrote in the post, "I love @taylorswift. Unfortunately, I am featured in her new documentary as part of a montage of a**hats saying mean things about her, which is used to explain why she felt the need to escape from the spotlight for a year. It’s insanely ironic because anyone who knows me knows I’m obnoxiously obsessed with her and her music. I first heard myself in the trailer last week as I watched it alone in bed (as soon as it came out bc I was so excited!) and I was horrified to hear my own voice."

She concluded, "In fact, her song “The Man” is the inspiration for my new hour of material and I feature the song is multiple iterations during my current tour. I love you Tay, and I can’t wait to watch 99.97% of your new doc.

Glaser was thrilled as Taylor Swift herself commented on the post, appreciating the apology.
