Dr Pimple Popper is back in 2022 popping corn kernels on patients heads Wed 16 Februar

TLC’s Dr Pimple Popper is back for season 7 in 2022. The series, which satisfies many people’s pimple-popping needs, returns to screens on February 16th at 9/8c. As well as the TLC show, Dr Pimple Popper regularly uploads pimple popping videos to her YouTube, Instagram and other social media channels.

For many it’s a love-hate relationship when it comes to the TLC show, you don’t want to watch but you also really do. So, let’s delve into Dr Pimple Popper’s corn kernels video from Valentine’s Day 2022 and also explore what’s to come in season 7 of her new series.

Dr Pimple Popper’s corn kernels video

While Valentine’s Day for many would mean bouquets of flowers and tables for two, Dr PImple Popper is uploading brand new content to her YouTube page which sees a patient have her ‘corn kernels’ squeezed out of her scalp.

Dr Lee writes in the video’s description that the technical term for the corn kernels is “A pilar cyst, also known as a trichilemmal or isthmus-catagen cyst, is a benign (non-life-threatening) growth that, upon examination, looks identical to the more common epidermoid cyst, except that 90% of pilar cysts occur on the scalp“.

She also writes that the cysts “can often be popped out whole, which not only ensures that I’ve removed it completely, but also leads to an extremely satisfying pop!”.

Dr Pimple Popper removes the pilar cysts from her patient’s head

During the February 14th video, Dr Pimple Popper removes the corn kernel cysts and says that she’s not scared by them as she removed “much bigger” before.

The almost eight-minute-long video sees the Dr say the pillar cysts are “quite cute” and takes two ‘corn kernels’ out of her patient’s scalp.

One of the kernels extracted reminded Dr Lee of a lychee and another she said was like a dim sum. Once she’d removed all of the cysts she stitched up the patient’s head.

The TLC show is back in 2022

Dr Pimple Popper fans will be pleased to know that the show is back for season 7 as of February 16th, 2022.

Episode 1, ‘Lookin’ for Love in Lumpy Places’, features Gabriel, Karen and Lorne who, between them, have growths, scaly feet and tumours for removal.

Watch the show at 9/8c on TLC and each Wednesday onwards!
