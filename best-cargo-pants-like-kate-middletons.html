Cargo pants are trending - here are 11 of our favourite pairs

The new decade has seen a whole lot of 00s trends coming back around, but one we were not expecting was the cargo pants resurgence. Practical and stylish, it actually makes a lot of sense they're once again a wardrobe staple. Especially as they can be worn sleek or oversized and styled with everything from Nike Dunks or Manolo heels.

Celebrities and royals wearing cargo pants

Bella Hadid, Hailey Bieber and Rosie Huntington-Whiteley have all been spotted wearing cargo pants. Even Princess Kate has made a case for cargo pants recently, wearing a G-Star Raw pair multiple times, from the Big Lunch during the Coronation weekend to visiting the Central Beacons Mountain Rescue Team in Wales.

We previously saw Kate wearing the same pair to spend a morning with the Irish Guards on Salisbury Plain in March and with a classic white T-shirt and safari shirt during her royal tour of the Caribbean in 2022.

A favourite of Rosie Huntington-Whitely, she's been seen looking so chic in a beige parachute pair with drawstring detail on Instagram.

Bella Hadid is basically synonymous with cargo pants. She wears her wide-leg pairs with chunky boots and loafers, and styles tapered pairs with sky-high barely-there heels.

Meanhwhile, cargo pants are an off-duty staple for Hailey Bieber, who always wears a wide-leg pair with chunky boots or trainers, from New Balance to adidas.

To wear the look for yourself, we've found all of the must-have pairs of cargo trousers available online.

How we chose the best cargo pants

  • Variety: As demonstrated above, cargo trousers are back in both wide-leg and skinny fits. We've found a mix of both, so whether you're more of a Kate or a Rosie, there's something for everyone. Many pairs suit both trainers and heels, so you'll get plenty of wear out of them.
  • Price: We've searched for cargo pants for all budgets, from affordable high street pairs at £29.99 to more premium forever pieces at £186.
  • Practicality: Part of the cargo pants appeal is how comfy and practical they are. We've chosen pairs in quality, lightweight fabrics, many of which have patch pockets and elasticated waistbands.
