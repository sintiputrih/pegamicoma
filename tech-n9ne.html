Tech N9ne Biography

Tech N9ne is best known as Rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, actor, entrepreneur who has an estimated Net Worth of $16 Million. Born Aaron Yates, he is an American rapper who is best known for his 2002 album Absolute Power. He was also a featured artist on the Lil Wayne single “Interlude.”  He was born on November 8,1971, Kansas City, MO. He co-founded the lable Strange Music in 1999. His stage name comes from the name of a handgun, the TEC-P semi-automatic, that he received as a gift from rapper Black Walt. He had an interest in the supernatural and often went searching for ghosts as a child. He was featured on the 1999 track “The Anthem” by Sway & King Tech, which also featured Eminem.  He has three children: Alyia, Reign and Dontez.  His zodiac sign is Scorpio
