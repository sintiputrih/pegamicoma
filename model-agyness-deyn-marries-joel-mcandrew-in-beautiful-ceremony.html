Model Agyness Deyn marries Joel McAndrew in beautiful ceremony

    Former model and actress Agyness Deyn looked stunning as she married her partner, hedge fund manager Joel McAndrew in a beautiful summery ceremony in Brooklyn Heights in New York City.

    The glowing bride looked stunning in a white and pale pink gown with ruffled sleeves which reached her ankles, revealing a pair of teal blue jewelled shoes. The 33-year-old carried a bouquet of white and brown flowers and kept her blonde bob straight, accessorising her look with a simple pair of teardrop earrings, while her delighted new husband wore a white suit with a blue shirt and tie, brown loafers and a pink corsage as the couple posed together.

    Agyness and Joel posed together after tying the knot 

    The couple's friend, fashion designer Henry Holland, shared snaps of the couple on their big day, writing: "So much [love] @aggy_deyn @joelmcandrew," before saying a close-up of the bride, accompanied by several bride emojis.

    The star had previously opened up about her upcoming wedding day, telling : "I've never been one for the fairytale, storybook wedding."

    The beautiful bride carried a bouquet of flowers and wheat on the big day

    Although Agyness has yet to share her own photos from her wedding day, she shared a candid snap of herself with Joel on the day of their wedding, writing: "I get to marry my best friend today."

    The actress was previously married to Giovanni Ribisi before their split in 2015, with revealing that the couple reached a settlement with Agyness keeping three of their homes, while Giovanni would keep two.

    Speaking about her past relationship, she told magazine: "I don't know. I suppose you meet someone and you're just like… you know. Obviously, if someone's marrying someone, they're bringing a lot of really good stuff."
