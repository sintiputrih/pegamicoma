FBG Butta Slams Adam22 For Calling Him A &quot;Zesty&quot; Snitch

Lately, FBG Butta's been making his rounds, claiming to have robbed Adam22 and No Jumper in various interviews. Adam22 has taken to social media to address the rapper's claims, denying that Butta actually took anything. The host went on to accuse him of snitching, noting that if he was able to lie about scamming him so easily, it's likely that he lied about that as well.

Adam threw a few jabs at the rapper in the process, claiming that whether or not he's "zesty" remains up for debate. He also said that he had a lengthy conversation with Butta recently, which saw him swear that he's an honest person. Adam appears to be confused by the entire situation, calling FBG Butta some kind of "sociopathic liar" and advising others not to believe a word he says.
