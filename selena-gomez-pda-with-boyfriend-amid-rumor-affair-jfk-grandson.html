Are Selena Gomez And Benny Blanco In Love?

Selena Gomez has previously dated Justin Bieber, The Weeknd, and Zedd. "I think most of my experiences in relationships have been cursed. I've been way too young to be exposed to certain things when I was in relationships," she told Vogue Australia in an interview in 2021.
