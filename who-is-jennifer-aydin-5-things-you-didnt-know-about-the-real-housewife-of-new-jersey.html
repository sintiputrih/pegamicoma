The Real Housewives of New Jersey: 5 things you didnt know about Jennifer Aydin! Wed 31 Octo

The Real Housewives of New Jersey are back!

The season 9 premiere will air at 8/9c on Bravo TV on November 7th.

Jennifer Aydin is one of two newcomers to season 9 of RHONJ. Season 9 looks to be drama-packed with a trip to Turkey, family feuds and rocky beginnings for the new housewives!

So who is Jennifer Aydin? 

Who is Jennifer Aydin? The Real Housewives of New Jersey – Bravo TV

Jennifer Has Five Children

Jennifer certainly has her hands full.

In The Real Housewives of New Jersey season 9 preview, Jennifer’s daughter is seen shouting: “Girls don’t do basketball! And I’m learning how to be a princess.”

Jennifer’s youngest daughter, Olivia, is 5-years-old and her eldest child is 13 years old.

Her youngest daughter has her own Instagram page.

Jennifer Has Housewife Friends

From her Instagram, it looks like Jennifer is already friends with Kathy Wakile who used to star in RHONJ, so she should fit right in.

Jennifer’s tagline for The Real Housewives of New Jersey is “I’m obsessed with family, traditions and Chanel.”

View Instagram Post

Jennifer’s Husband is a Plastic Surgeon

Jennifer is married to Dr Aydin, a cosmetic surgeon who works in New Jersey.

Jennifer said on the RHONJ preview: “I was in the process of being arranged for marriage to somebody else, but I didn’t like the person that my parents were picking for me. But when I saw my husband it was like a lightbulb.”

Dolores Catania said she used to work as a surgical assistant for Jennifer’s husband.

Dolores said: “I guess New Jersey is a place where plastic surgery brings everyone together.”

Jennifer is of Turkish Descent

In an interview with Bravo TV, Jennifer said that she used to have her own Turkish cooking show which can be found on YouTube. (Jennifer’s Kitchen)

This season the housewives head to Turkey for belly-dancing, traditional cuisine and camel rides.

In the RHONJ preview, Teresa shouted: “My camel toe is getting a camel ride!”

Jennifer Aydin Grew Up Around Bling

Jennifer has said on her Bravo TV interview that she grew up in the jewellery business.

After college, she opened up her own jewellery store.

But, when Jennifer became a full-time mother she closed the jewellery shop.
