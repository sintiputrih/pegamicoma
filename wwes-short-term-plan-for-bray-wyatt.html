WWEs Short-Term Plan For Bray Wyatt

Bray Wyatt’s new Fiend character has really registered with fans in a big way and it looks like we’ll be seeing him a lot more.

The thing about an appearance from The Fiend is that it can be over in an instant. It’s like a very quick and violent thing that can swoop down at any time.

It appears that Bray Wyatt will be showing up consistently on Raw until the end of October. Bray Wyatt is advertised for every episode of Raw through October 28th, but he could be around more often than that.

Wyatt is currently slated for a Universal Title shot at Hell In A Cell on October 6th. He will either be facing Braun Strowman or Seth Rollins. Depending on how things go, we could be seeing the title go a much different Superstar soon enough.
