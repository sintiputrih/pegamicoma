Professional Life

Professionally, Ladye Wanda Jimenez a.k.a Chilly Jimenez is an American YouTuber. She initially gained fame as a former partner of the YouTube gamer Logan Thirtyacre.

She has portrayed several characters in the SuperMarioLogan series on her husband’s YouTube channel. Similarly, she plays Cecilla in the Summer School series, Peach in Mario’s Wig, and Pink Yoshi in Mario’s New Girlfriend.

Likewise, she also has a solo channel on YouTube titled ‘Chilly.’ She created the channel on April 15, 2008. She began with pet videos in which she featured her kitten.

Quickly, she began doing vlogs alongside her husband Logan. On January 4, 2014, the couple posted the first video together under the title ‘New York City Vacation’.

On March 8, 2017, she shared ‘A Vlog and Behind the Scenes’ which became an instant hit. Next, she posted another video titled ‘Ruined Our Couch- behind the Scenes’ that too became successful.

In April 2017, she came out with a prank video. The following video showcases their holiday theme and it went on to garner a moderate number of views.

American YouTube personality continued sharing new and exciting videos and eventually became famous on YouTube. Currently, she has managed to earn over 2.2 million subscribers.

Some of the notable videos on her channel are ‘Jeffy the Rapper Behind the Scenes ‘, ‘Destroying Jeffy Puppet Prank’ and ‘Tito Come Outside “music video” By SML’.

The first video showcases a “behind the scenes” video with around 11 million views while the second one is about a prank video with more than 16 million total views.

Likewise, the third one is a music video by her then-husband Logan with around 13 million views.
