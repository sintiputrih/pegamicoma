I See You (2019): Where Was the Movie Filmed?

Directed by Adam Randall, ‘I See You’ is a 2019 horror thriller movie that revolves around the sudden disappearance of a 12-year-old boy, whose case is led by investigator Greg Harper. Already having troubles with his wife, Jackie, he struggles to cope with the investigation’s pressure. As strange occurrences plague the house, Jackie’s sanity dangles, and her grip on reality weakens. However, when an actual masked persona lets his presence known and puts their son, Connor, in danger, she and Greg must try to find out who lurks in the shadows behind a mask.

Featuring impressive onscreen performances from a talented ensemble comprising Helen Hunt, Jon Tenney, Judah Lewis, Owen Teague, and Libe Barer, the crime movie unfolds in the small fictional town of Edgecliff, especially inside the Harper household, as the dark visuals reflect the presence of something evil as well as the volatile relationships of the family members. Thus, many of you might find the sites featured in the movie quite intriguing, leaving you curious to know where ‘I See You’ was filmed. In that case, let’s hunt down the places together, shall we?

I See You Filming Locations

‘I See You’ was filmed entirely in Ohio, specifically in Cuyahoga County, Vermillion, and Auburn. As per reports, the principal photography for the thriller movie commenced in May 2018 and wrapped up in about three weeks or so, in early June of the same year. So, without further ado, let us take you to all the specific sites that stood in for the fictional town in the Adam Randall directorial!

Cuyahoga County, Ohio

To portray the fictional town of Edgecliff, the production team primarily set up camp in Cuyahoga County and utilized suitable locales across the region. As mentioned above, the lakefront Harper household is where most of the action takes place, and a real-life property at 15404 Edgewater Drive in the city of Lakewood was used to lens the sequences. As for the scenes involving Tommy’s home, they were reportedly shot in and around 1078 Holmden Avenue in Cleveland. Moreover, the backdrop of Lake Erie is quite prominent throughout the movie.

During the filming schedule, the cast and crew members of ‘I See You’ were spotted taping several scenes in Cleveland, including the area near the Steel Mills and at 2169-2105 West 3rd Street. The village of Chagrin Falls also served as a key production location for the thriller, with the Main Street Bridge on North Main Street featuring in a few scenes. In addition, the production team utilized the locales of Solon, a city in southeastern Cuyahoga County, to shoot pivotal portions.

Other Locations in Ohio

The filming unit of ‘I See You’ also traveled outside Cuyahoga County to other parts of Ohio to tape additional portions for the movie. For instance, key scenes were lensed on location in Linwood Park at 4920 Liberty Avenue in the city of Vermilion. Furthermore, the premises of Sirna’s Farm & Market at 19009 Ravenna Road in Auburn Township are featured in the thriller movie.
