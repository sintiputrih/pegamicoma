Career

  • Wood made his county cricket debut for Northumberland against Norfolk in the MCCA Knockout Trophy in 2008. From 2008 to 2010, he represented Northumberland in Minor Counties cricket, appearing in three Minor Counties Championships and three MCCA Knockout Trophy matches.
  • He made his Durham debut in a first-class match against Durham MCCU during the 2011 season. He then made his List A debut against Northamptonshire in the 2011 Clydesdale Bank 40. He has subsequently played another first-class match against Sri Lanka A[5,] as well as another List A match against Scotland in the Clydesdale Bank 40. Wood earned his England Lions debut during their 2014 tour of Sri Lanka.
  • Wood was acquired by the Chennai Super Kings for INR 1.5 crores (£160,000) on January 28, 2018. In the auction for the 2022 Indian Premier League, he was purchased by the Lucknow Super Giants in February 2022. He was forced to withdraw from the event owing to an elbow ailment.
  • He was purchased by the London Spirit in April 2022 for the 2022 season of The Hundred.

  • On May 8, 2015, he made his One-Day International debut for England against Ireland. Rain disrupted the match, resulting in little cricket being played, but Wood did take his maiden international wicket.
  • Later that month, he made his England Test debut against New Zealand.
  •  Wood got 3-93 in New Zealand’s opening innings in the first Test. He then collected 1-47 in their second innings to help England win by 124 runs and take a 1-0 series lead. Wood continued to excel in the next Test, capturing 2-62 in New Zealand’s opening innings. He also hit a valuable 19 with the bat. He grabbed three more wickets in New Zealand’s second innings, this time for 97 runs. England defeated by 199 runs, and the series was tied at 1-1.
  • Wood took 1-48 in the third One-Day International against New Zealand. In the next game, he took 1-49 to help England win by seven wickets and even the series at 2-2. Despite the fact that Wood did not take a wicket in the last game of the series, taking 0-70, England won the series 3-1.
  • On June 23, 2015, he made his Twenty20 International debut in the same series. He ended with 3-26 as England triumphed by 56 runs.
