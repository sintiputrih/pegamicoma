Who Is Camilla Colbro? Her Bio, Husband, Surgeon, Age, Net Worth

Reality House Season 3 dropped on YouTube on 9 November 2021. As you all know the twist in the show is that ten creators live in a huge Big Brother-type house hoping to win $100K. Now, let us introduce you to model and mom Camila Colbro, one of the contestants on the season this time.

This ‘Camilla Colbro Bio’ mostly includes her husband, age, job, family-related stories, and more.

Camilla Colbro On Reality House

So, Camilla Colbro got invited to be on the Reality House because of her fame as a model working under DT Model Management and DIRECTIONS USA, as a social media influencer, and as someone who went viral on TikTok sometime ago.

Before finding an audience on TikTok, she worked as a professional model; she continued modeling after.

With that being said, it is still too soon to tell you what dramas Camilla has found herself in while inside the Reality House this season.

Who Is Camilla Colbro Husband?

On her Facebook profile, Camilla Colbro have it writing that she has been married since 11 November 2010. Meanwhile, her husband Terrance Coleman Brooks’s relationship status read ‘single’.

However, based on what she said on a TikTok video back on 25 February 2021, Camilla is still together with her husband and they are having the best time raising their kids.

Camilla explained that she has been together with Coleman since she was just 15. Replying to a TikTok user who said that her kids and husband were going to be like “WTF” after she had forehead surgery done, Camilla strongly gave her back that her husband fully supports her decision about the surgery.

Camilla shares two kids, a son and a daughter with her husband. Their daughter Owen Natalia Coleman Brooks turned 5 on 26 November 2021.

About Camilla’s husband: as per LinkedIn, Terrance is a transitioning US army soldier, working full-time as a supply specialist as of March 2018.

A year before joining the army, he worked as a meat clerk at Whole Foods Market. In the past, he also did other varied jobs of a warehouse worker, line cook, dishwasher, and such.

Terrance is from Elizabeth, New Jersey, and went to Abington Senior High School.

Camilla Colbro Forehead Surgery

Camilla Colbro’s very first viral TikTok has been watched over 36 million times, where she says that she has always been insecure about how tall her forehead is — and that, the “flaw” made her always wear her hair in bangs to cover it.

Following this, she kept posting such very vulnerable videos on TikTok, and finally one day she had enough money to undergo plastic surgery to make her forehead smaller (reduce it by 3cm).

Later, she showed images of herself swaddled and healing.

Camilla Colbro Net Worth

It has been reported that Camilla Colbro spent $6,958 (£5,000) on her forehead surgery. From this, one can speculate that she does well financially and must have made a significant sum of net worth.

How Old Is Camilla Colbro?

Born on 16 February 1995, Camilla Colbro turned 26 years old in 2021. Also, her zodiac sign is Aquarius.

Camilla Colbro Family

Camilla Colbro’s mother Tara Colquitt is on Instagram @thecreditwoman. According to her profile on it, she has been helping people obtain mortgages, business loans, and overall financial health since 2007.

Camilla also had good times growing up with her brother Jibri Colquitt (on Instagram @_jibri) who is now married to a beautiful wife and is a proud father of two kids.
