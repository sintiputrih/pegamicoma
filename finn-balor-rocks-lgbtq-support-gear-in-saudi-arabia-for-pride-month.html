Finn Balor Rocks LGBTQ Support Gear In Saudi Arabia For Pride Month

Finn Balor might have traveled to Saudi Arabia to work a match at the WWE Super ShowDown, but he was in full support of the LGBTQ community the entire time. Because Balor Club is for Everyone.

The Demon Kind defeated Andrade in a match where they really gave their all. It was unbelievably hot in the ring in excess of 100 degrees, but it didn’t slow them down. However, the crowd might not have been too into it considering that they were a bit tired by that point.

Before the Super ShowDown event kicked off, Finn Balor took a picture of himself in front the big set while wearing an LGBTQ pride shirt with a caption saying:

Love is love. Happy Pride Month from Saudi Arabia!

The sad truth is that there isn’t much gay pride in Saudi Arabia. There is unspeakable horror waiting for those who come out of the closet in that country and it is painful to consider that such a large portion of the world is like that.

It’s also a very good idea that Balor waited until after he was already on his way out of the Kingdom Of Saudi Arabia before posting this picture too.
