Brittany Daniel Bio (Wiki)

Who is Brittany Daniel?

Brittany Daniel is an American film and television actress. She is popular for her role as Jessica Wakefield in the 1950s syndicated teen drama ‘Sweet Valley High.

Brittany is also well recognized for her role as Kelly Pitts on the CW/BET comedy-drama series ‘The Game’. She is the twin sister of the photographer and former actress Cynthia Daniel.

How many siblings does Brittany Daniel have?

Brittany Daniel was born on March 17, 1976, along with her twin sister Cynthia Daniel in Gainesville, Florida, USA. Cynthia is five minutes younger and one inch taller than her.

Her birth name is Brittany Ann Daniel and she is currently 46 years old. Her father’s name was Charlton Bradford(‘C.B.’) Daniel Jr. and her mother’s name is Carolyn.

Her father was running for Mayor of her hometown of Gainesville, Florida, but he died in 2008 due to cancer. Daniel has got an older brother named Brad. She along with her twin sister were signed to the Ford Agency and they began modeling.

Brittany Daniel is an adventurous woman and she loves rock climbing and rollerblading. Daniel holds American citizenship but her ethnicity is unknown.

Brittany Daniel: Education, School/College University

Talking about her education history, she has attended Gainesville High School.

Brittany Daniel: Professional Life and Career

Brittany Daniel began her carer as a model and appeared in ‘Seventeen’ and ‘YM’. Also, both of them appeared in ads for Doublemint dum as the ‘Doublemint Twins’.

Daniel debuted her acting career with the appearance in the sitcom ‘The New Leave It To Beaver in 1989. She won the role of Mila Rosnovsky at the age of 16. She portrayed the role in the teen drama ‘Swan’s Crossing’ and then moved to New York to film the series.

After completing high school, Brittany Daniel has portrayed the role of Jessica Wakefield in the television series ‘Sweet Valley High’ and made her film debut in the drama ‘The Basketball Diaries’ in 1995 alongside Leonardo DiCaprio.

In 1999, Brittany Daniel made her appearance on ‘Dawson’s Creek’ and appeared in the movie ‘On Hostile Ground’ by the following year.

In addition, Brittany Daniel made her appearance in ‘That ‘70s Show’, ‘It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia’, ‘White Chicks’, ‘Little Man’, ‘The Game’, ‘Skyline’, and many more.

Brittany Daniel: Awards, Nominations

Brittany Daniel won YoungArtist Award in the category Best Performance: Young Actress in a TV Comedy Series for ‘Sweet Valley High’ in 1995, was nominated for Young Artist Award in the category Best Young Actress in an Off-Primetime Series for ‘Swans Crossing’ in 1993, and nominated several times in her career.
