Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott Go to Disney World with Stormi

  • Kylie Jenner and ex-boyfriend Travis Scott were spotted hanging out together at Disney World with their daughter, Stormi Webster.
  • The trip was reportedly a pre-birthday excursion for the former couple's daughter, who is turning two years old next week.

Kylie Jenner and ex-boyfriend Travis Scott continue to be co-parenting goals while out and about on a Disney World trip with their daughter, Stormi Webster.

The former couple were spotted hanging out at the amusement park yesterday afternoon, making it their first public outing as a family since Scott’s film premiere of his Netflix documentary, Look Mom I Can Fly, back in August.

According to TMZ, the Disney World trip was an early birthday gift to Stormi, who is turning two next week. Kylie’s mom, Kris Jenner, and older sister Kourtney Kardashian were also in tow for the birthday festivities. Though the two are committed to continuing friendly co-parenting for their daughter, they have no plans to get back together anytime soon, according to the outlet.

Aside from a daytime Disney visit, Kylie is set to celebrate her daughter this Valentine’s Day as well with a specially curated makeup collection dedicated to the toddler. The makeup mogul teased the collection on her Instagram, showcasing Stormi-approved, pastel-hued, butterfly-adorned packaging; gorgeously glimmering eye shadows; and mini lip glosses.

“I think I’ve been waiting for this moment since the day I found out I was pregnant…,” the proud mom captioned the accompanying teaser clip of the collection. “An entire collection by @kyliecosmetics dedicated to my beautiful daughter. I can’t wait for the reveal!!!! You will fall in love.”
