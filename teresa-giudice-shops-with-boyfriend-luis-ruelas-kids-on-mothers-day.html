Teresa Giudice shops with boyfriend Luis Ruelas, kids on Mothers Day

Teresa Giudice’s love language must be shopping.

The “Real Housewives of New Jersey” star was spotted on a Hamptons shopping trip on Mother’s Day with her boyfriend, Luis Ruelas, and three of her daughters, Gia, 20, Gabriella, 17, and Milania, 15.

A source exclusively told Page Six the couple were out East since that is where Ruelas’ mother and sisters reside.

As the crew strolled through swanky Sag Harbor, Ruelas — dressed sharply in a blue suit jacket and slacks — held onto three stuffed bags from the high-end clothing store Flying Point Surf Boutique.

Teresa was dolled up beside her man wearing black leather pants, a blazer and platform heels.

Ruelas shared on Instagram a picture of his mom and sisters with Teresa, captioning it, “Happy Mother’s Day to these 4 beauties LOVE YOU ?.”

He then added a sentimental note to his lady, writing, “If I I could give you one gift right now it would be the ability to see yourself thru everyone’s eyes and only then would you realize how special amazing and LOVED you are by everyone.”

The enamored “Housewife” responded, “Babe you take my breath away. I adore ? you endlessly.”

On her Instagram, Teresa celebrated by calling her daughters “my best gift ?,” and explained that her youngest, Audriana, could not attend the Mother’s Day celebration because she “had dance.”

The reality star later shared a picture from the 12-year-old’s competition and captioned it, “Audriana spending Mother’s Day with you was such a joy seeing you perform on stage.”

Teresa’s ex — and Audriana’s father — Joe Giudice commented, “She is so adorable ??.”

A source told Page Six Teresa and Ruelas also paid their respects to Teresa’s late mother, Antonia Gorga, at a cemetery while in New Jersey. She died at age 66 in 2017.

Teresa and Joe split in December 2019 after 20 years of marriage. The former couple finalized their divorce in September 2020.

Page Six first revealed in November that Teresa and Ruelas were an item.

Despite allegations that the New Jersey-based businessman is “sex-obsessed” and has a playboy past, he appears to be hitting it off well with Teresa’s daughters.

In April, Ruelas took his son Louie Jr. for a night out in New York with Gia and Milania, while Teresa filmed a “Housewives” all-star spin-off show.

Fellow “RHONJ” star Melissa Gorga has said she thinks Ruelas and her sister-in-law will get married one day.

“I don’t know everything there is to know about love, but I do know when two people go out of their way for one another, they can’t take their eyes off each other, and she totally lets her guard down with him,” she said in a confessional interview. “And that’s how I really know that it’s the one.”

A rep for Teresa did not immediately return our request for comment.
