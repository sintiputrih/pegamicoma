Daniel Padilla Biography

Daniel John Ford Padilla, popularly known as Daniel Padilla, is a Filipino actor and recording artist. He is a talent of ABS-CBN and Star Magic, and currently stars as Gino in the primetime Filipino drama Princess And I.

He hails from one of the most popular clans of Philippine showbiz, being the son of actor Rommel Padilla and actress Karla Ford (more known as Karla Estrada). Because of his strong resemblance to his uncle Robin Padilla who became famous for his “bad boy” image, he is often tagged with the same image.

He Has a band called “Parking 5” with his brother Jose Carlito “JC” Padilla the Vocalist, and his best friends Katsumi Kabe the Rhythm Guitarist, Lester Giri the Drummer and Seth Gothico the Lead guitarist and he is the bassist of Parking 5 that started in 2010.He likes playing basketball,Skateboard and Playing music with his band.Their band Play Rock Songs.

Daniel Padilla Career

 YOUTH-ORIENTED SHOWS

Padilla’s first acting stint was in 2010 when he starred in a minor role in Gimik 2010, a remake of the hit 90’s Filipino youth-oriented show. His character Daniel Ledesma was paired to teen actress Julia Montes.

Growing Up, another youth-oriented show by ABS-CBN, was considered to be Padilla’s big break. He reportedly replaced his real life best friend Albie Casiño who was originally given the role as Kathryn’s leading man in Mara Clara.

DEBUT AS A PRIMETIME ACTOR AND SINGER

After two consecutive Sunday youth-oriented shows, Padilla finally landed a role in a primetime television series in Princess And I alongside his previous show’s team-up partner, Kathryn Bernardo. He currently plays Gino dela Rosa, a character whom he describes as “a swaggering rich kid who will change and sacrifice a lot for the sake of love”.The romantic-comedy royalty-themed series, which is currently airing, is doing well in the ratings, having already reached the 40% mark and placing second behind Walang Hanggan.

On May 28, 2012, Padilla released his debut self-titled Album, Daniel Padilla. The Album has six tracks with the carrier single Hinahanap-hanap Kita which is also featured in his primetime showPrincess And I. According to Star Records producer Jonathan Manalo, Daniel’s debut Album has sold more than 10,000 copies in less than a week. On June 17 of the same year, Padilla was given a Gold Record Award for the sales of his debut album.

Most notable success of Padilla’s showbiz career are the stampeding crowd of people in his mall shows and the his many fans ranging as young as 4 and as old as 80 years old. He’s usually a trending topic on the social networking site Twitter. He’s also one of the endorsers of BUM, a clothing line in the Philippines, among others. Boy Abunda, a Filipino talk show host and TV personality, has been quoted into saying that “In 5 years, Daniel Padilla is going to be one of the biggest stars of Philippine showbiz”.

Kathryn Bernardo doesn’t want Daniel Padilla as leading man for Julia Barretto

MANILA, Philippines — Kapamilya actress Kathryn Bernardo does not see Julia Barretto as a suitable leading lady for on- and off-screen partner Daniel Padilla.

In last week’s episode of “Tonight With Boy Abunda,” Kathryn told Abunda, the show’s host, why she feels Daniel and Julia do not look good together.

“Feeling ko too young. Parang masyado nang bata for DJ, kasi ngayon, medyo pa-lolo na siya eh,” Kathryn said.

Kathryn was in the show to promote her first movie with Kapuso star Alden Richards, “Hello, Love, Goodbye,” to be showing in cinemas starting Wednesday (July 31).

During the movie’s recent press conference, Kathryn shared what really happened behind the viral video showing Daniel surprising her in Hong Kong.
Kathryn also narrated how she coped without having a mobile phone and a means to communicate with Daniel for about a month, when the movie’s director, Cathy Garcia-Molina, confiscated her phone to make her feel the pain needed to more effectively portray her character in the film.
